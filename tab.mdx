Pictogram fully supports [TAB](https://github.com/NEZNAMY/TAB) through our [PlaceholderAPI integration](placeholderapi). 

Because **TAB** has native support for **PlaceholderAPI**, you can seamlessly use your registered Pictogram images in the tablist, scoreboards, bossbars, and nametags.

<Warning>
  To use Pictogram with TAB, you must have **PlaceholderAPI** and **PacketEvents** installed, and packet formatting must be enabled in your Pictogram config.
</Warning>
