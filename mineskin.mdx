Pictogram wouldn't exist without [Mineskin](https://mineskin.org). The technology they provide is the backbone of how Pictogram renders images in-game, and without them this plugin simply wouldn't be possible.

If you'd like to support them, you can do so at [account.mineskin.org/store](https://account.mineskin.org/store). On top of helping keep the project alive, supporting them also gets you higher rate limits, which means your images will generate faster when reloading Pictogram.
